`SecResponseBodyAccess`
`SecResponseBodyMimeType`
`SecResponseBodyLimit`
`SecResponseBodyLimitAction`

`SecUploadDir`
`SecUploadKeepFiles`
`SecAuditEngine`

`SecAuditLogType`
`SecAuditLog`
`SecAuditLogParts`
`SecCookieFormat`
`SecRequestBodyInMemoryLimit`
`SecDebugLog`
