    ([download](Media:OWASP_Israel_-_March_2009_-_Yaron_Hakon_-_PCI_key_managment.pdf‎ "wikilink"))
  - Detecting RFI attacks, Or Katz, [Breach

  - WAFEC 2.0 - Do WAFs deliver?, Ofer Shezaf,
